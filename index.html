How it works

Simple by design.

BCWA runs on Jabrium — a free, voice-first platform. You tap, you talk, your members hear you. No algorithm in between.

The magic of Jabrium is speech to text and intelligent AI agents listening in the background, ready to step in when needed. You don't interrupt anybody and they don't interrupt you. They read your words and respond accordingly. The conversation continues — the pace doesn't matter. Everybody is heard. Nobody is left hanging.
1

Sign up and tell us about your group.

Name, email, phone number, and a little about your Facebook community. Takes less than a minute. We'll text you right away — no waiting, no sales call.

2

Talk to your BCWA agent.

A dedicated agent learns about your group and your goals. Through voice conversation — not forms, not menus. Just talk. The agent will help you figure out the best way to bring your members off-platform.

3

Before you invite anyone, your agent walks you through the member experience. You'll know exactly what your community is stepping into — because your reputation matters.

4

Invite your members. In your words.

Share a link in your Facebook group — written by you, in your voice, the way your members know you. They tap, they join, and you have a real, persistent conversation with each of them.
